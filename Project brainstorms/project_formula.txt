
Formula for text-as-data research 


What question do you want to answer? This should be something you *actually* want to know, and it should end in a question mark.

To what extent do sports announcers rely on cliches in sports and entertainment?


What source of text data will you use?

I really don't know, I'm sure there are some transcripts of things they say. 

What are two possible answers to the question?

Either announcers frequently use the same words and phrases or they frequently used a diverse and varied vocabulary.

How will the results of the quantitative text analysis demonstrate which of those answers is correct?

The results may show the frequency of certain words and phrases by the amount of time used . If the same ones are repeated constantly it could prove they rely on cliches.
